Shortcuts and gestures

Here is a lift of some shortcuts and gestures you might find useful.

Table 1. Shortcuts and Gestures
Function Gesture
Zoom In Pinch 2 fingers outwards
Zoom Out Pinch 2 fingers inwards
Undo Tap screen with 2 fingers
Continuos Undo Hold down with 2 fingers
Redo Tap screen with 3 fingers
Continuos Redo Hold down with 3 fingers
Rotate Twist with 2 fingers
Eye Dropper Tap & hold 1 finger on color
Erase Layer Wiggle 3 fingers
Normal Size Pinch 2 fingers closed
Cut/Copy/Paste Swipe down with 3 fingers
Full Screen Tap with 4 fingers

How to activate drawing guides

Drawing Guide provides a basic grid over your canvas in order to help you with proportions.

Before you begin you need an open project in Procreate with some content on it.

Add a visual guide to your canvas to help you build realistic environments and objects.

  1. Tap the Actions icon.
  2. Tap on Canvas.
  3. Toggle the swith on the Drawing Guide tab.
A grid composed of squares will appear over your artwork.
The drawing guide is perfect to use when you are painting a photorealistic piece and need spot on proportions.
